HL: Accommodation


Karma Borgo di Colleoli, a resort in Tuscany has evolved since the late Middle Ages, first as a castle and later as a villa for Tuscany’s aristocracy during the Renaissance. In 2003, it was meticulously restored by Palummo & Pierotti Architects under the guidance of the Italian Fine Arts Commission. The historic villa now houses the Karma Borgo di Colleoli Palace, offering 12 luxury suites that celebrate its rich heritage.
Keywords: Karma, Renaissance, Italian Fine Arts Commission, resort in tuscany
