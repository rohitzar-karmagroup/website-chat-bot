Web Intro (max 120 words):
Set in on a hilltop in the verdant outskirts of Carcassonne, Karma Château de Samary is a testament to Southern France's architectural heritage: an authentic 19th-century château given a sophisticated contemporary makeover that honours its history while delivering a luxury experience to guests. Outside, a terrace gives onto expansive gardens and a secluded pool terrace perfect for sun-drenched relaxation. Each thoughtfully appointed space preserves authentic character while providing premium comfort one of the jewels of southern France - all just a few short minutes from the iconic citadel of Carcassonne. 


Keywords: Karma, hilltop, Southern France, historic, 19th-century, heritage, hotel in Carcassonne France
