HL: Accommodation
Karma Golden Camp, a luxury camp in Jaisalmer, spread over 3.5 acres in the Thar Desert, features an amphitheater, private dining venue and a stargazing deck. The All-Day-Dining Restaurant, ‘Khejri,’ serves desert-inspired Rajasthani cuisine made from locally sourced ingredients using traditional methods. The Three Monkeys Club offers activities like board games, table tennis, badminton, volleyball and mini cricket.
Guests can enjoy nightly performances by local folk artists, including kalbeliya dance, classical Sufi, and Marwari songs and activities like camel rides and dune bashing. With 26 uniquely styled units, including cottages and tents, the accommodation adds charm to your desert holiday experience at Karma Golden Camp.
Keywords: Karma Golden Camp, Rajasthani cuisine, Thar Desert, luxury tent in Jaisalmer
