HL: Cuisine 
At Karma Sitabani, indulge in the classics of North Indian cuisine, from rich Biryani rice dishes to Uttarakhand specialties like Aaloo ke Gutke, spiced potatoes often served at festivals and Phannu, a fragrant lentil stew. Experience authentic country fare, away from the hustle and bustle of the city.
Keywords: Karma Sitabani, North Indian cuisine
